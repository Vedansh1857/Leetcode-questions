Problem statement -> A chef has collected data on the satisfaction level of his n dishes. Chef can cook any dish in 1 unit of time.
Like-time coefficient of a dish is defined as time taken to cook that dish including previous dishes multiplied by its satisfaction level i.e. time[i]*satisfaction[i].
Return the maximum sum of like-time coefficient that the chef can obtain after dishes preparation.

Problem link -> https://leetcode.com/problems/reducing-dishes/description/
